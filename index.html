About Me

K Kiran is a highly accomplished Senior Technical Officer - III at the CSIR - Indian Institute of Chemical Technology (IICT), Hyderabad. With a strong academic background including an M.E., he is a distinguished Fellow of IEI, Fellow of IETE, and a Senior Member of IEEE.

His expertise spans cutting-edge domains including Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Automation & Control, and Bioinformatics. Mr. Kiran is dedicated to leveraging AI to solve complex scientific and industrial challenges, contributing significantly to innovative research and technological advancements.

He is known for his work in applying artificial neural networks for various classification and identification problems, demonstrating a deep commitment to impactful scientific inquiry.
